The St. Thomas Marthoma Church Marathahali Choir continued to be an integral part of spiritual wellbeing of the parish. Since the inception, the choir has been part of the communal worship and inspiration to many believers. As the most revered wing of the parish, the choir strives to achieve comfort to the sick and less fortunate. In addition, we help the worship celebrant during Holy Communion through praise and worship.

The choir has been our comfort during many events including marriage, death and social events. It also participates in regular worship services and holds countless hours in practices. Some of the main activities of the choir include Choir Sunday, Get-togethers, Choir Retreats (both Sr. Choir and Jr. Choir), Christmas Carols, Easter Recitals, Outreach programs, etc.

We hope and pray that the Lord Almighty continue to bless and guide us for many years to come.
