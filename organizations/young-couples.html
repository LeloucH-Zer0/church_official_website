The Young Couples Fellowship ( YCF ) was initiated with the objective of engaging with parish members who felt that they had out grown the Youth League and were not close to the senior Citizens category as well. These were Parishioners who wanted to continue to lead an active life at the church and contribute to building the church with the resources at hand. The pre-requisite to be part of this group was to be married and there was no cut off age prescribed .

Activities

  • YCF is providing monthly home provisions to the AIDS affected families who are undergoing treatment at the Hoskote Mission Hospital as part of YCFs monthly aid support
  • YCF Fellowship meetings are conducted on 2nd Saturdays @ 7pm every month . This is a session that is being planned for all members of the YCF .
  • YCF members actively involve and participate during Harvest Festival by providing live food counters and stalls for kids /families
  • Christmas Tree and Chruch decorations are done by YCF members with the support of Youth league during the month of December
