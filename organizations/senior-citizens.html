The Senior Citizens’ Fellowship of St. Thomas Mar Thoma Parish, Marathahalli Church was formed to gather all members above the age of 60 for their fellowship meetings and other support activities. Many of this group are the founding members of STMC parish and have strong emotional connect to various activities. Of late, the members usually meet in Zoom platform on the 4th Saturday evening of every month. Prayer, Bible reading, testimony, messages from eminent personalities representing spiritual and other subjects of common interests are amongst the activities in these meetings. 20-25 members participate in our regular zoom meeting. Senior Citizens Day is celebrated on the 3rd week in September. We intend to actively participate in Sunday Worship and to invite a Senior retired clergy for sharing the word of God. We have donated 4-wheel chairs for the handicapped children. We are also planning to get involved in few other charitable activities during the year 2020-21.
