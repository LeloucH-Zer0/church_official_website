The Mar Thoma Syrian Church, Bengaluru

The parish's history traces back to 1917 AD when Syrian Christians gathered for worship and fellowship at the United Theological College (UTC) Bangalore. The Bangalore Mar Thoma Parish became a reality in 1945 with Rev. Philip Oommen (Most. Rev. Dr. Philipose Mar Chrysostom Valiya Metropolitan) as the first vicar.
